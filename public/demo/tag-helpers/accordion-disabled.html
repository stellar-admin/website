What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and popular digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay for your convenience.
When will I receive my booking confirmation?
Your confirmation and e-tickets are usually emailed within minutes. If not, please check your spam folder or view the details in your account's "My Trips" section.
Can I earn loyalty points or frequent flyer miles?
Yes, you can enter your frequent flyer number during flight booking. For hotels and packages, eligibility depends on the specific service provider's loyalty program rules.
